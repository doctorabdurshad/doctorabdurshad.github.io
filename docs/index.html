Internal Medicine

Comprehensive treatment for all systems of the body, focusing on prevention, diagnosis, and management of a wide range of medical conditions including:

  • Chronic diseases (diabetes, hypertension, cholesterol)
  • Thyroid and hormonal disorders
  • Heart and lung conditions
  • Digestive and kidney disorders
  • Obesity and metabolic disorders
  • Acute illnesses and infections
  • Annual physical
  • Employment physical
  • School physical

Pulmonary Diseases

In addition to providing education and guidance, we diagnose and manage a wide range of diseases related to the lungs and airways, including but not limited to:

  • Upper airway and lung conditions (sinus, airway, and pleural diseases)
  • Disease related to airborne exposure at work or at home
  • Occupational and environmental lung diseases
  • Viral, bacterial, fungal, and parasitic lung disease
  • Tuberculosis
  • Lung nodules and lung cancer evaluation
  • Asthma and COPD
  • Interstitial lung diseases
  • Lung complications related to other medical conditions

Smoking, Vaping, & E-cigarette Cessation Counseling

Counseling is available for patients who want to quit smoking, vaping, or using e-cigarettes. This service is designed to help you better understand the effects these habits have on your lungs and overall health, and to support you in making informed decisions about quitting.

During counseling, we will review how smoking and vaping impact the respiratory system and other parts of the body in a clear and understandable way. You will also receive personalized guidance on effective cessation strategies, recommended resources, and ongoing support throughout your journey towards better respiratory health.

Telemedicine

Since the COVID-19 pandemic, telemedicine has become a safe and effective way for patients to receive medical care without needing to leave the comfort of home. We are pleased to offer secure video visits so you can connect directly to us while maintaining peace of mind and convenience.

If you are experiencing symptoms such as chest discomfort, nasal congestion, a lingering dry cough, shortness of breath, or other breathing concerns, a telemedicine visit may be the right first step. During your appointment, we will carefully listen to your symptoms, review your medical history, and provide guidance or treatment recommendations. If further in-person evaluation is needed, we will help you arrange the appropriate next steps.

Preoperative / Presurgical Evaluation

If you are preparing for an outpatient surgery, we are here to help you move through surgery and recovery with confidence. We will carefully review your respiratory health, discuss any concerns you may have, and perform simple tests if needed. This helps us understand your individual risk level and ensures that your surgical team has all the information they need to keep you comfortable and safe.
