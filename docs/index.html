Telemedicine

Since the COVID-19 pandemic, telemedicine has become a safe and effective way for patients to receive medical care without needing to leave the comfort of home. We are pleased to offer secure video visits so you can connect directly to us while maintaining peace of mind and convenience.

If you are experiencing symptoms such as chest discomfort, nasal congestion, a lingering dry cough, shortness of breath, or other breathing concerns, a telemedicine visit may be the right first step. During your appointment, we will carefully listen to your symptoms, review your medical history, and provide guidance or treatment recommendations. If further in-person evaluation is needed, we will help you arrange the appropriate next steps.

Preoperative / Presurgical Evaluation

If you are preparing for an outpatient surgery, we are here to help you move through surgery and recovery with confidence. We will carefully review your respiratory health, discuss any concerns you may have, and perform simple tests if needed. This helps us understand your individual risk level and ensures that your surgical team has all the information they need to keep you comfortable and safe.
